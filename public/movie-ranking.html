The Best Movies According to Nani

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pan's Labyrinth

I really like the fantasy aspect of this movie. It's really sad, but interesting how the main character escapes her reality.

Spawn

I really love the comic book character Spawn. The movie was so good and I loved it as a kid.

Robinhood Men in Tights

This is such a funny movie to watch. So many dumb jokes, but I grew up with this and always quote it with my siblings and best friend.
